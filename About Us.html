Our Mission and Objectives

We, the parents, S.G.B., educators and non-teaching staff fully commit and dedicate ourselves to the satisfaction of the basic academic and extra-curricula needs of our learners throurgh effective effective rendering of such services, that will lay a sound foundation for their future academic and extre curricula development and success.


We intend to attain this through:
  1. The improvement and maintenance of available physical resources.
  2. Optimum utilisation and development of available human resources.
  3. Acquisistion of educational aids such as textbooks, learning aids, equipment, etc.
  4. Erection of laboratory and media centre.
  5. Improvemnt of administration through the acquisition of a computer, in order to ensure the safe-keeping of accurate and reliable information.
  6. Acquistion of equipment for sports and cultural activities selected, and incorporation of extra-curricula programmes
