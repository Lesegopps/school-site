Registration Information Required Documents Admission Criteria Financial Obligations

Lesego Public Primary is bound by the South African Schools Act (1996) and North West Department of Education policies:

  • Admission cannot be refused based on race, language, religion, or financial standing. Education is compulsory from Grades 1â€“9, or until the child is 15 years old.
  • Our school is no-fee paying. Registration and admission are FREE.
  • Grade R: 5 turning 6; Grade 1: 6 turning 7 in the year of admission.
  • Application forms are available at the school office. Parents are advised to complete as much as possible and consult staff if needed.

Required documents for admission:

  • Completed Application form
  • Certified copy of Parent's ID or Passport
  • Proof of residence (Municipal Rates letter)
  • 3 wallet-sized pictures of the child
  • Certified copy of the child's birth certificate
  • Transfer letter and progress report (if applicable)

Admission criteria:

  • Age: Must meet Grade R/1 age requirements; otherwise, admission may be deferred.
  • Incomplete Application: Missing documents must be submitted when requested, or the application may be dismissed.
  • Special Needs: All learners are admitted irrespective of ability. Limited resources will be addressed collaboratively with parents.

Financial obligations:

  • Grade R graduation fees
  • Farewell expenses
  • Civvies Day

All other registration and admission processes are free of charge.
