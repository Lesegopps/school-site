About Us

Built on humanity, pride, initiative, community, respect and hope, our school aims to produce and be the cream of the crop. We may be guided by our ethical principles and legislative obligation, but are driven by the desire to be the firm foundation our nation's future requires. Our school strives to be a fortress of academic rigour, communal belonging and individual development for each and every one of our learners. We want nothing but the best, and work every single day to make it happen.
